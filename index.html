    Intelligent Calculation Methodology:
    Deviation = Forecast-to-Now volume vs actual volume to date (from Verint).
    Net Δ Workload = (ΔVol × Forecast AHT) + (Actual Vol × ΔAHT).
    FTE impact uses shrinkage-adjusted productive hours, occupancy-adjusted via Erlang proxy.
    Projection blends run-rate vs plan using a sigmoid confidence score on elapsed shift fraction.
